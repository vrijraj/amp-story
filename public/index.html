Google Developers Group

Google Developer Groups (GDGs) are for developers who are interested in Google's developer technology.

WomenTechmakers

Google's Women Techmakers program provides visibility, community, and resources for women in technology.

Goolge Developers Expert

A global program to recognize individuals who are experts and thought leaders in one or more Google technologies. These professionals actively contribute and support the developer and startup ecosystems around the world, helping them build and launch highly innovative apps.

Developer Student Clubs (DSC)

Developer Student Clubs (DSC) are community groups for students from any academic background in their undergraduate or graduate term.
